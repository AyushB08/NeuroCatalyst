Our Mission

Our Services

Parkinson's Awareness

At NeuroCatalyst, we have taken notice to the lack of awareness about Parkinson's. This is why we will raise Parkinson's awareness through our workshops, events, and other community outreach programs.

Application/Research Development

At NeuroCatalyst, we are developing applications that can assist Parkinson's victims in their day-to-day lives. We believe that technology can be a powerful tool in the fight against Parkinson's disease. For the tech we develop, we will also publish research papers.
