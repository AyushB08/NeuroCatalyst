Our Services

Parkinson's Awareness

At NeuroCatalyst, we have taken notice to the lack of awareness about Parkinson's. This is why we will raise Parkinson's awareness through our workshops, events, and community outreach programs.

Financial Assistance

We understand that Parkinson's can take a significant financial toll on families. That's why we will provide financial assistance and discounted medicines to Parkinson's victims and their families, helping them get the care and support they need.

Algorithm Development

Our team will be dedicated to developing algorithms that can assist Parkinson's victims in their day-to-day lives. We believe that technology can be a powerful tool in the fight against Parkinson's disease. We also believe that expanding knowledge regarding Parkinson's is important so we publish research papers too.
