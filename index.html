Our Services

Parkinson's Awareness

At NeuroCatalyst, we have taken notice to the lack of awareness about Parkinson's. This is why we will raise Parkinson's awareness through our workshops, events, and community outreach programs.

Financial Assistance

We understand that Parkinson's can take a significant financial toll on families. That's why we will provide financial assistance and discounted medicines to Parkinson's victims and their families, helping them get the care and support they need.

Algorithm Development

Our team will be dedicated to developing algorithms that can assist Parkinson's victims in their day-to-day lives. We believe that technology can be a powerful tool in the fight against Parkinson's disease. We also believe that expanding knowledge regarding Parkinson's is important so we publish research papers too.

Our Mission

NeuroCatalyst is driven by immense feelings of care for those who suffer from Parkinson's; we are devoted to making their lives easier by creating helpful new tools and standing up for them. By crafting specific algorithms we aid Parkinson's victim's daily lives. We understand all of the big and small struggles Parkinson's patients encounter and their drive to find new ways to improve their daily lives. In addition, our team at NeuroCatalyst understands how essential it is to provide financial aid and to spread awareness among communities to inform people about Parkinson's. We aim to ease the money worries of those fighting against the stigma attached to the disease and the disease itself all while making sure the voices of the impacted are heard loud and clear. We are dedicated to building a more caring and helpful world for anyone affected by this disease.

Make a Real Difference

By becoming part of our team, you'll be able to make a tangible impact on the lives of Parkinson's patients who are struggling financially. Your contributions will directly help those in need by providing financial assistance for treatments, meds, and quality-of-life resources.

Raise Awareness

Parkinson's disease isn't always understood or talked about as much as it should be. By joining us, you can help change that by raising awareness about the challenges patients face and the importance of early detection, access to treatment, and support. Your efforts will help educate communities and create more understanding and empathy for people living with Parkinson's

Lead Innovation

We develop innovative new algorithms and tech solutions to help Parkinson's patients manage their symptoms and live better. As a member, you'll get to be at the forefront of innovation, working on projects that could totally transform the lives of millions of people with Parkinson's disease.
